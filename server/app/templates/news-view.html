About the Computer Science Department

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at Delta State Polytechnic, Ogwashi-Uku, located in the vibrant state of Delta, Nigeria. Our department is a hub of innovation, learning, and technological advancement in the heart of the region.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset necessary to excel in the ever-evolving field of computer science. We strive to instill a deep passion for technology and provide an environment where creativity and problem-solving flourish.

At the Computer Science Department, academic excellence is our hallmark. Our dedicated faculty members are experienced professionals and scholars who are committed to delivering top-quality education. We offer a wide range of programs, from diploma courses to higher national diploma (HND) programs, designed to meet the needs of aspiring computer scientists.

We are not only dedicated to teaching but also to pushing the boundaries of knowledge through research. Our faculty and students engage in research projects that tackle real-world challenges in the field of computer science, contributing to advancements in areas such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software development, and more.

We look forward to connecting with you and embarking on a journey of discovery and innovation in the world of computer science.
